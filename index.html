About this app

Safe minimum internal temperatures shown here are based on published USDA/FDA guidance. Ground meats have a higher safe minimum than whole cuts of the same animal because grinding spreads any surface bacteria through the meat. Poultry (including ground poultry) has a higher safe minimum than red meat.

Doneness levels above the safe minimum, and all oven / smoker / grill time estimates, are common enthusiast and smoking-community reference points — not regulatory figures. Actual cook time always depends on thickness, bone-in vs. boneless, starting temperature, and your specific equipment. Always verify with an instant-read thermometer.

This app is for personal reference only and is not a food-safety authority. When in doubt, consult usda.gov.
